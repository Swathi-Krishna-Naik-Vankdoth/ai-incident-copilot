ESCALATION POLICY - INCIDENT RESPONSE
======================================

SEV-1 (Critical) - Full outage or data loss
- Response time: 15 minutes
- Owner: On-call Engineering Lead
- Actions: Page on-call engineer, notify VP Engineering, start war room
- Stakeholder update: Every 30 minutes
- Escalate to: CTO if unresolved after 1 hour

SEV-2 (High) - Major feature broken, no workaround
- Response time: 1 hour
- Owner: Team Lead + Product Manager
- Actions: Assign engineer, notify Director, post in #incidents Slack
- Stakeholder update: Every 2 hours
- Escalate to: VP Engineering if unresolved after 4 hours

SEV-3 (Medium) - Minor issue, workaround exists
- Response time: 4 hours
- Owner: Assigned engineer
- Actions: Create ticket, notify team lead
- Stakeholder update: Daily
- Escalate to: Team Lead if unresolved after 2 days

LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED FOR:
- Any change affecting user data or privacy
- New third-party partner integrations
- Changes to terms of service or billing
